Playing online Games

Eleazar / 10 April 2024 / Games

I can have more time to communicate with friends and family members. Even though some online games allow you to chat with other gamers, it is simply the same as we communicate with others when we are doing outside activities.

I don’t agree that online gaming is a waste of money because that money can also help the developer of the game optimized it more.

When I play games, I always don’t notice the time because I’m having fun playing, with or without someone. Also, it can lessen my stress and makes me comfortable to do the this needed to accomplish just like my academic projects and activities.
